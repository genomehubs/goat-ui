# Genomics of the Brazilian Biodiversity (GBB)

**GoaT Search Term:** GBB

**Scope:** _High-quality reference genome sequencing_

::grid[**GBB** The GBB is a Consortium funded by Vale and is under the coordination of both Vale Institute of Technology and ICMBio, which aims to the genomic mapping of Brazilian fauna and flora species that are threatened with extinction, invasive exotics, or that have bioeconomic potential. For more information reach the contact info below or access the [GBB project page](https://www.itv.org/en/genomics-of-the-brazilian-biodiversity-gbb/).]{size=8}

**Bioproject ID** PRJNA1180976

**Main Contact**: [Alexandre Aleixo](alexandre.aleixo@itv.org)

- long_list=GBB
