#  Genomics of Huntable Species in France (HUNTomics)

**GoaT Search Term:** HUNTOMICS

**Scope:** _High-quality reference genome sequencing_

::grid[**HUNTomics** focuses on French game species, primarily mammals and birds. Led by the Fédération Nationale des Chasseurs (FNC), the project coordinates sampling, sequencing, and assembly efforts to produce high-quality reference genomes. It also generates genomic datasets for conservation genomics studies, including demographic inference. For more information access the organization [website](https://www.chasseurdefrance.com/etudes-scientifiques/) or reach the contact info below.]{size=8}

**Bioproject ID** PRJEB86906

**Main Contact**: scientifiquefnc@chasseurdefrance.com

**Project Status** Active
