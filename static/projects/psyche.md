# Summary Data

## PSYCHE Long List

PSYCHE long_list correspond to all species of butterflies listed in the [Lepiforum's Checklist of European Lepidoptera](https://lepiforum.org/wiki/page/Downloads). To retrieve the list use the search terms:

- long_list=PSYCHE
- tax_rank(species)
